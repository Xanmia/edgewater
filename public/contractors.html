Steps for Contractors

Thank you for your interest in working with Edgewater Investments, LLC. We will be assisting with the processing of your background application and meeting the requirements of your Edgewater Contractor Agreement.

Below are the required forms and outlined the process, feel free to contact us if you have any questions!

  • Please send a copy of your valid Driver’s License, Social Security Card, Vehicle Registration, and Proof of Auto Insurance.
  • Please complete the CrimShield Authorization Form, Direct Deposit Form, and W-9 (to include both your SSN and your FEIN)
  • Please complete the Edgewater Subcontractor Agreement. Please keep a copy for yourself and the original will need to be mailed to the office address below or given to you Edgewater supervisor.
  • You will also need to send a scan or copy of your Worker’s Compensation Certificate and General Liability Certificate.
  • In the following week will need a Photo for your badge that meets the following requirements: from the shoulders up, White Background ONLY, no hats or sunglasses
All of these items should be emailed to me at sgreer@edgeteck.net andtmurray@edgeteck.net. Please include your best contact phone in your response as well.
